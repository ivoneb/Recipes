Barramundi with Salsa Verde and Asparagus

INGREDIENTS:
	1 ½ tbs olive oil
	4 vine cherry tomatoes
	4 large parsley leaves
	4 x 150g skinless barramundi fillets
	8 slices prosciutto
	Steamed asparagus, to serve

SALSA VERDE:
	1 cup chopped flat-leaf parsley
	2 tbs chopped mint
	2 tbs chopped basil
	1 tbs baby capers, drained
	2 anchovy fillets
	2 cloves of garlic, peeled
	¼ cup sweet gherkins, drained
	1 tbs red wine vinegar
	1 tbs Dijon mustard
	160ml extra virgin olive oil

METHOD:
1. To make salsa verde, process all ingredients, except oil, until finely chopped. With motor operating, gradually drizzle in oil until smooth. Season.
2. Heat 2 teaspoons of the oil in a small frying pan. Cook tomatoes, turning, until soft and just beginning to collapse. Season.
3. Place a parsley leaf on top of each fish fillet. Wrap each fillet in two slices of overlapping prosciutto.
4.Heat remaining oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add fish. Cook, for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until prosciutto is crisp and fish is cooked through.
5. Serve fish with asparagus, tomatoes and salsa verde.

RATINGS:

	* * * *
	4 STARS!!
